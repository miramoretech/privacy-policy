MiramoreNow Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

MiramoreNow ("we", "our", or "us") operates a multi-service platform offering food delivery, shopping, event planning, AI assistance, and other digital services across Nigeria.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your personal data is collected, used, and safeguarded.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address

b. Usage & Device Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address
  • App usage activity and interactions

c. Transaction Information

  • Orders placed (food, shopping, services)
  • Payment references (handled securely via third-party providers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send service updates and notifications
  • Personalize features (including Mira AI assistant)
  • Detect and prevent fraud or misuse

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share your information with:

  • Vendors and service providers
  • Delivery/logistics partners
  • Payment processors
  • Law enforcement when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

MiramoreNow may use trusted third-party services such as payment systems, analytics tools, and AI services (including Mira AI). These operate under their own privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Children's Privacy

MiramoreNow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
